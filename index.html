About Us

Reliable trims, made to keep
production lines moving.

ALPHA BD PACKAGING is reliable and experienced in manufacturing and supplying Readymade Garments (RMG) Accessories — components that enhance, complete and decorate garments. The company has been operating successfully for 8 years.

Core products include sewing thread, elastic band, twill tape, drawstring & drawcord, printing items, and buttons. Robust infrastructure and skilled workforces are dedicated to providing superior quality garment accessories at affordable prices, ensuring timely delivery, and maintaining a strong focus on social and ecological concerns.

Our team is dedicated to building strong relationships based on trust, reliability, and mutual growth.

  • Timely delivery and commitment
  • Well-experienced work team
  • Accreditation by world reputed teams
  • OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certified production

Manufacturing Process

From raw material
to ready shipment.

  1. 01

    Material Sourcing

    Raw yarns, fabrics and components sourced against buyer specifications and certification requirements.

  2. 02

    Production

    Dyeing, weaving, knitting, printing and moulding across our factories in Chattogram and Dhaka.

  3. 03

    Quality Control

    In-line and final inspection against colour, strength and dimensional tolerances before packing.

  4. 04

    Packaging

    Carton labelling, poly bagging and carton packing prepared to each buyer's shipping standard.

  5. 05

    Delivery

    Scheduled dispatch aligned to your production line and shipment date — on time, every time.
